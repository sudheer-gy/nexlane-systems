Full SCADA & Data Modernization for a Multi-Line Production Facility

A Connecticut tobacco manufacturer was running 14 SQL Server historian databases in FULL recovery mode with no log backups since 2021 — a single failure away from losing years of production data. Operator screens crashed on export. Quality data lived in Access files. Leadership had no visibility into energy costs or batch performance.

NexLane Systems stabilized the entire data infrastructure, rebuilt the Ignition Vision screens, automated daily compliance reporting, and delivered a live solar + grid energy dashboard to the executive team — all within the first 90 days on site.
